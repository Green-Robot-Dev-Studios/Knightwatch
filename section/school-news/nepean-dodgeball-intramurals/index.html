Nepean Dodgeball Intramurals
anonymous /  Thu, 15 Dec 2022

On December 9th 2022, the first intramural competition of the 2022-2023 year was held, as students faced off against each other in multiple games of dodgeball inside the gymnasium. The Intramurals are held once every year. Senior Link Students host multiple friendly tournaments every week for different sports each time.


The first game of this year was dodgeball, and the next week it was badminton. For more information on how to enroll for these events, their venues, and more, please visit the Nepean StudCo Instagram page (@nhsstudco).


The winning team of this week's dodgeball tournament were handed a random prize for their victory. It was an enjoyable event to watch and to participate in despite losing or winning. “I did have a lot of fun participating in the tournament today, and it was super easy to join, we just had to gather 8 players to be eligible to play at lunch and we did win so I’d say it was worth it”, said a player.


For those who are not very active on social media, multiple posters can be found almost everywhere around the school, so once again, for more information on these events, look out for posters wherever you go.
